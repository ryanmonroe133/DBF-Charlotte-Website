## Best Image Sizes

- Hero images: 1920 x 1080 or larger
- Section images: 1200 x 800 or larger
- Card images: 800 x 600 or larger

## Notes

- Use `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, or `.webp`.
- Avoid spaces in filenames.
- If your files have different names, that is fine. Tell Codex the filenames and which slot each one should replace.
